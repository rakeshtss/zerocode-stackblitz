Permission Process Filming in Maharashtra


The Film Facilitation Office Maharashtra is the point of contact for filming in Maharashtra. The Film City Mumbai regularly engages with the nodal officers in each state of India to ease out the process of permissions.

REGISTER

Register yourself with the Film City Mumbai website to start your application process.

FILL APPLICATION FORM

Fill out the application form for seeking permission for film shooting in India.

UPLOAD DOCUMENTS

Upload script, synopsis of the script, passport details of the crew, details of shooting locations in India & period of shooting.

PAY FEES

An application fee of INR equivalent to USD 225 needs to be paid online. The fee is non-refundable.

SUBMIT APPLICATION FORM

Submit the form on your own or through an Indian Representative / Line Producer.

Unique Locations Unlock Your Narrative

Maharashtra, a land of stories and a melting pot of emotions, narrates itself through breathtaking landscapes. It expresses itself via age-old, man-made splendours like the Taj Mahal, the mythological narratives at the Ghats of River Ganges, and the cultural clans of the Seven Sisters in the North-East of India. Little wonder then that films shot in India pulsate with life. Coming to India, a filmmaker comes home to his imagination.

Why Film in Maharashtra?


Explore a vast eco-sphere of film making resources at competitive prices in the Indian subcontinent.

CULTURAL RICHNESS

Among the oldest cultures in the world, Maharashtra is a diverse mix of numerous languages, cuisines, identities, religion, art, ethnicity and geography.

PRODUCTION RESOURCES

Maharashtra has a large talent pool of actors, producers, directors, writers & experienced crew including competent line producers across the country.

FILMING HUBS

A diverse range of production and post - production facilities pan Maharashtra with multiple well - equipped film production hubs.

READY AUDIENCE

Maharashtra,with its ever growing appetite for films, offers a ready audience that loves watching their location in International films.
