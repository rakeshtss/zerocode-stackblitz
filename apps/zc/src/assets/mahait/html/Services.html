  • Location Booking
    • Maharshtra Film Cell is a one-stop solution to co-ordinate filming permission in Maharashtra. Locations play a crucial role in amplifying the attractiveness of the film shots, the key function of Maharshtra Film Cell is to offer seamless film shooting location booking for all the locations developed, maintained and owned by state government departments.
    • Through Maharshtra Film Cell online system you can directly book any locations listed on the portal and can also place a request to add new locations on the system. It also offer an overview of key locations across Maharashtra under different categories.
  • Subsidies and Incentives
    • Government of Maharashtra has started a scheme to provide financial incentives to Marathi film producers as incentive to produce quality Marathi Films in the year 1997.
    • The films are scrutinized by the film Screening committee and awarded category:A & B.
      • Category A Film is eligible for an incentive of Rs.40 lakhs
      • Category B Film is eligible for an incentive of Rs.30 lakhs
    • The Film which are found of neither 'A' or 'B' Category will not be eligible for any incentives
    • Application Process
      • Fill application form
      • Pay application fee (online)
      • Submit and download application form
      • Submit documents along with application form at Filmcity office Hyperlinked to Download tab (List of document for Subsidy Application)
    • Apply for subsidy (link to subsidy application form)
