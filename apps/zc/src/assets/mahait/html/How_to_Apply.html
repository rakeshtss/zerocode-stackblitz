How to Apply


Single Window Facilitation Cell set up by MFSCDC acts as a facilitation point for the film producers and production companies to get requisite permission, guide and inform the film makers on shooting locations and the facilities available in the state.

Create Account

Create your account on MFC portal to start your application process.

Fill Application Form

Fill out the application form and all the mandatory details.
Location details, time and date of shooting

ADD LOCATION

Fill in location details, time and date of shooting

UPLOAD DOCUMENTS

  • Upload all the mandatory documents (as applicable)
    • User Registration: Govt. Approved ID proof, Passport/Visa in case of foreigners
    • Production house profile documents: Company registration certificate, GSTIN, TAN/PAN
    • Project Profile Documents: Gist of script, MIB/MEA certificate in case of international films & documentaries, concerned approval from central departments

Pay Fees

An application fee to paid online, to enable submission of your application.

Terms & Condition

Accept Terms & Condition specified by MFC and stakeholder departments

SUBMIT APPLICATION FORM

Submit the form on your own or through an Indian Representative / Line Producer.
